# MeatRoaf

&tags bork family, beef, warm, meal
&source 

## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb ground Beef
- 1 egg
- 1/2 c salted crackers
- 1/4 c ketchup
- 1/2 tsp onion, diced

## Equipment

- Baking dish

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees F
1. Prepare onion

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients in a baking dish until well combined
1. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes

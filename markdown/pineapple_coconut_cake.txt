# Pineapple Coconut Cake

&tags bork family, dessert, sweet, cake
&source 

## Ingredients

- 16 oz angelfood cake mix
- 24 oz crushed pineapple
- 1 tsp coconut extract
- 1 tsp pineapple extract
- 8 oz reduced gat cream cheese
- 2 tbs powdered sugar
- 1 1/2 c reduced fat whipped topping
- 1/4 c toasted coconut

## Equipment

- Tube pan

## Preparation

- Preheat oven to 350 degrees F

## Instructions

1. Combine angelfood mix, pineapple, and coconut extract, then beat lightly for 30 seconds
1. Beat mixture on medium speed for 1 minute
1. Pour cake mixture into cake pan, then bake for 35-40 minutes, or until cake is springy
1. Invert pan and allow to cool completely, then transfer cake to serving plate
1. Beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and pineapple extract until smooth
1. Gently fold whipped topping into frosting mixture
1. Frost cake, then top with toasted coconut

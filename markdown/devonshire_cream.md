# Devonshire Cream

&tags bork family, dessert, component, cool

## Ingredients

- 2 cups sour cream
- 2 tbs powdered sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Equipment

- Whisk or stand mixer

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients and whisk

## Notes

Can be made up to 2 days in advance.
